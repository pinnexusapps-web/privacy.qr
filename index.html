Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 17, 2025

Privacy-First QR Generator

We don't collect, store, or track any of your personal data.
Your privacy is our priority, and our app is designed to work entirely on your device.

1. Our Commitment to Privacy

QR Generator, developed by PINNEXUS Apps, is designed with privacy as a fundamental principle. We believe that generating QR codes should be a private process that doesn't require sharing your personal information with us or anyone else.

Zero Data Collection: We do not collect, store, transmit, or have access to any of the content you use to create QR codes or any personal information.

2. How Our App Works

QR Generator processes all QR code generation locally on your device. This means:

  • Local Processing: All QR codes are generated directly on your device using client-side technology
  • No Server Storage: Your data never leaves your device or gets sent to our servers
  • No Account Required: You don't need to create an account or provide any personal information
  • Instant Generation: QR codes are created in real-time without any network communication

3. What We Don't Collect

To be completely transparent, here's what we explicitly do NOT collect:

  • The content you input to generate QR codes (text, URLs, contact info, etc.)
  • The QR codes you generate
  • Your IP address or location data
  • Device identifiers or browser information
  • Usage statistics or analytics data
  • Personal information of any kind
  • Cookies or tracking data

4. Third-Party Services

Our app operates independently and does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. We do not use:

  • Google Analytics or similar tracking services
  • Social media plugins or tracking pixels
  • Advertising networks
  • Cloud storage services for your data
  • External APIs that would transmit your data

5. Data Security

Since we don't collect or store any user data, there's no risk of your information being compromised, leaked, or misused. Your data remains entirely under your control on your device.

For your own security when using QR codes:

  • Be cautious when sharing QR codes containing sensitive information
  • Verify QR code content before sharing with others
  • Keep your device secure with appropriate passwords and updates

6. Children's Privacy

Since our app doesn't collect any data, it's safe for users of all ages, including children under 13. Parents can feel confident that no personal information about their children is being collected or stored when using QR Generator.

7. International Users

Our privacy-first approach means that users from any country can use QR Generator without concerns about international data transfers, GDPR compliance, or other regional privacy regulations, since no data is collected in the first place.

8. Open Source Transparency

We believe in transparency. If you're technically inclined, you can verify our privacy claims by:

  • Inspecting the app's network activity (you'll see no data transmission)
  • Reviewing the source code (if available)
  • Using browser developer tools to confirm local processing

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

Since our fundamental approach is to not collect data, changes to this policy would only occur if:

  • We need to clarify our existing privacy practices
  • Legal requirements necessitate additional disclosures
  • We add features that might affect privacy (which we would clearly communicate)

Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

10. Verification of Our Claims

We encourage users to verify our privacy claims:

  • Network Monitoring: Use browser developer tools to monitor network requests
  • Offline Testing: Try using the app without an internet connection
  • Local Storage: Check that no data is being stored locally without your knowledge
